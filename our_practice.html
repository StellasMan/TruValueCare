About Us:

At Tru-Value Care, we are committed to providing compassionate, evidence-based healthcare that supports individuals in living healthier, more fulfilled lives. We believe that emotional wellness is essential at every age — and everyone deserves care that honors dignity, independence, and personal goals. Our practice is led by a board-certified psychiatric mental health and adult-geriatric nurse practitioner with many years of specialized clinical experience caring for patients of different age groups. We offer professional evaluation, diagnosis, medication management, and wellness support to our patients. We are dedicated to helping our patients maintain the highest possible quality of life — with respect, compassion, and the dignity they deserve.

Whether in-person in the Houston/Katy area or through secure telehealth statewide, we provide personalized care designed to help you heal, grow, and regain control of your wellbeing.


Our Mission

To empower individuals and families through accessible, high-quality health care that promotes mental, emotional, wellness, and spiritual well-being.

About

Ngozi Kalu, PMHNP-BC, AGNP-C, is a board-certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner and Adult-Geriatric Nurse Practitioner with over 15 years of extensive clinical experience. She is dedicated to delivering compassionate, evidence-based care that supports individuals in achieving emotional stability, improved daily functioning, and long-term wellbeing.

Ngozi has spent a significant portion of her career caring for the adult and geriatric population, including individuals living with chronic medical conditions, cognitive decline, and age-related mental health challenges. Families and caregivers consistently value her calming approach, her respect for dignity in aging, and her commitment to improving quality of life.

Ngozi welcomes both in-person and virtual patients, creating safe spaces where collaboration and trust are foundational to care. She specializes in the treatment of various medical conditions, such as hypertension, diabetes, anxiety, depression, insomnia, and trauma-related disorders. Through personalized treatment planning, she ensures that both patients and families play an active role in the healing journey.

Her clinical approach combines comprehensive medical evaluations, thoughtful medication management, and supportive counseling, tailored to meet each person’s unique needs, goals, and level of independence. She believes that true healing happens when care is accessible, respectful, and grounded in partnership.
