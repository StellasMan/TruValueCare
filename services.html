Our Services include

  • Medication Management
    Evaluation, diagnosis, and ongoing management of psychiatric medications tailored to your unique needs.

  • ADHD Evaluation & Treatment
    Thorough ADHD assessments. Treatment includes medication options, behavioral planning, and lifestyle structure coaching.

  • Anxiety Disorders
    We treat generalized anxiety, panic disorder, social anxiety, and PTSD-related anxiety. Treatment combines medication support, coping strategies, and functional improvement.

  • Depression & Mood Disorders
    Support for major depressive disorder, persistent depressive disorder, postpartum depression, and seasonal depression, including medication and lifestyle guidance.

  • Bipolar Disorder
    Evidence-based mood stabilization, monitoring, safety planning, and structured follow-ups.

  • Trauma & PTSD
    Grounded and compassionate trauma care with medication support and coping skills development.

  • Insomnia & Sleep Disorders
    Evaluation of underlying causes and tailored treatment plans to restore healthy sleep.

  • Telepsychiatry
    HIPAA-compliant telemedicine accessible from your phone or laptop.
