About

Dr. Tanyi is a board-certified Family Medicine Physician who has been in practice for over a decade. She has served as a medical director and physician leader.

Education

Dr. Tanyi obtained her medical degree from Ross University School of Medicine and completed her Family Medicine residency at the University of Oklahoma Health Sciences Center.

Experience

Dr. Tanyi has a wide scope of practice and extensive experience in multiple disciplines, including primary care, behavioral health, urgent care, occupational health, integrative medicine, and more. As a Family Medicine Physician, she has treated mental health disorders for over a decade. Dr. Tanyi practices in Houston, TX, and resides with her family in Richmond.
